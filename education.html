DIPLOMA IN INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

Finishing my 2017 SPM, I was offered to continue my studies in Diploma Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM). Kedah branch. I recognised from the beginning that I had to abandon my former life that was spending time with my home village and being remote from my relatives. But when I can adapt the new culture to my new area immediately, I observed that I am delighted because it teaches me how to survive on my own as well as to learn new things. Today, as we all know, the new concern which is the pandemic Covid-19 that affects our country's whole sector has to be addressed. Sadly, we have to adjust to modern culture, which is to be furthered through online medium studies. We must thus accept what happened and attempt to discover other means of continuing our graduation journey. This is my last semester at UiTM Kedah Branch, even in the last three half, utilising just the online media.
